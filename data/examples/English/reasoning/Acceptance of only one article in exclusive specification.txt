A creature is an animal, fungus, human-being, micro-organism, or plant.
An animal is a vertebrate or invertebrate.
A vertebrate is an amphibian, bird, fish, mammal, or reptile.
An invertebrate is an annelid, arthropod, echinoderm, mollusk, or protozoan.
